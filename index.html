About Us

Roblox British Airways is a non-profit virtual airline, founded in October 2024, operated by volunteers and supported by aviation enthusiasts from all over the world. It allows members to express their passion for aviation within a safe environment. Our goal is to build a community of welcoming, knowledgeable individuals looking to simulate realistic flights in a friendly environment on the PTATC Network.

Rest assured that we're here for the long run, as a heavily invested, well-established organization. Not requiring any experience outside, but with a majority of our team members having experience in both real world and simulation, be sure you'll receive the best possible assistance from educated individuals.
